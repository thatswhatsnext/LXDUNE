Unit Learning Outcomes (Linked to Assessment)

LO1 Curriculum Knowledge
Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of the concepts, substance and structure of content and teaching strategies across Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Investigating Science, Earth & Environmental Science and Science Extension syllabi.
GTSD 2.1.1
LO2 Assessment Knowledge
Demonstrate understanding of assessment strategies (diagnostic, formative, summative; informal and formal) and the importance of reporting student progress and effective home–school communication.
GTSD 5.1.1, 5.4.1
LO3 Investigative Skills & Safety
Include and evaluate a range of teaching strategies that develop investigative skills, cognitive engagement, and safe scientific practice in senior secondary contexts.
GTSD 3.3.1, 4.1.1, 4.2.1, 4.4.1
LO4 ICT & Resource Evaluation
Implement teaching strategies that use ICT to expand learning opportunities and critically analyse and evaluate digital and other teaching resources.
GTSD 2.6.1
LO5 Professional Resources & Networks
Demonstrate knowledge of professional resources and critically analyse textbooks and other resources to enhance learning opportunities.
GTSD 3.4.1, 7.4.1, 3.6.1
LO6 Critical Textbook Analysis
Demonstrate ability to critically analyse a textbook and provide a range of appropriate resources and activities to address identified shortcomings.
