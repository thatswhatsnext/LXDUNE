Topic 8 — Teaching with a Diverse Cohort

This week, we turn to one of the most important and complex aspects of teaching: designing learning for the diverse range of students in your classroom. In senior science, we have a professional, legal, and moral obligation to ensure that all students can access learning. Your role as a teacher is to plan experiences that are both rigorous and inclusive, enabling every student to engage meaningfully with the content.

This Week's Focus

Topic 8 focuses on how teachers design inclusive learning experiences in senior secondary science. This includes differentiation, adjustments, and planning approaches that enable all students to engage with complex scientific ideas.

A key idea in this topic is that inclusion is not something that happens after planning — it is something that is designed from the outset.
